User Research for Klimatkoll webbapplication

For the development of the KlimatKoll app, user research was crucial to understanding the needs and behaviors of elderly users regarding environmental issues. The goal was to create a product that was both accessible and engaging for users who may have limited experience with digital technology.

Based on the research findings, we simplified the navigation by reducing the number of menu options and incorporating larger, more readable fonts.
The app's main page was redesigned to include clear, actionable steps for learning about sustainability, paired with illustrations to make the content more engaging.
